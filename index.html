About us

At LuaSoft Rentals, our story is rooted in a deep love for travel and the desire to create unforgettable memories for our clients. Founded by three young professionals who share a passion for exploring the world and curating exquisite experiences, LuaSoft was born out of a genuine love for hospitality.

Our Stays

LuaSoft Stays brings to our guests a quality travel experience. We offer apartments, lofts, and vacation homes to professional travelers in various locations. Our stays provide hotel-like amenities, such as travel-size toiletries, towels, & premium linen for every stay, along with all the advantages of a home, like spacious kitchens and comfortable living spaces. Our guests find comfort, quality, and safety at every stay. Our local teams at each location work hard to maintain a consistent quality experience for each guest. LuaSoft is available 24/7 via text or phone call to answer any questions, concerns, or accommodations. Are you looking to stay with us for a month or longer? We welcome you home. LuaSoft also offers 30+ night stays to accommodate the corporate/business traveler, long-haul vacationer, and digital nomad. We are located in Maryland and Virginia and welcome travelers for extended stay accommodations with our fully-furnished apartment suites that seamlessly integrate work and play while providing all the comforts of home.
